    Beginner's Middle Eastern Percussion

    Fridays:
    Beginners - 6:30 - 7:30 pm
    Intermediate - 7:30 - 8:30 pm

    Drop-in: $5.00 per class, or $8.00 for both classes
    10 Week Class Card: $40.00 Beginner, or $70.00 for both classes

    Please call or email to register for this class

    Class will include introduction to Middle Eastern percussion instruments and basic, standard rhythms. Core frame and doumbek technique will be a strong focus of the class. Class will include music theory as it pertains to the standard rhythms. It is highly suggested that you bring a Middle Eastern frame drum (tar/def) or doumbek (no djembes please). There will be a couple of extra frames for drop in students, however those taking the entire session will be expected to provide their own instrument.

    All classes are held at Devyani Dance Center
