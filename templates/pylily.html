To generate a score image, type in the time signature (2/4, 4/4, etc) followed imemdiately by a comma and the rhythm in Dtk notation. For example, the rhythm Ayuub would be entered as "2/4,D-_kD-T-" (excluding the quotes). Note that dotted notes, such as the D-_ at the beginning of Ayuub should be entered as the beat, followed by the extention of the beat (-) and a rest (_).
